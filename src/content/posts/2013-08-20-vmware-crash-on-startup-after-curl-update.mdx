<p>After today update of debian Sid VMWare keep crashing on startup without any interesting log.</p>

<p>. The crash it’s caused by the curl update that changed the lib interface.</p>

<p>There are two ways of solving this: downgrade Curl (i.e. using snapshot debian) or applying a patch that force vmware to use its own libcurl instead of the system one.</p>

<p>Thewe are the 2 patches, one for vmware and one for vmplayer:</p>

```
--- /usr/bin/vmware-original 2013-02-09 18:22:36.194459631 +0100
+++ /usr/bin/vmware 2013-02-09 18:22:45.234459997 +0100
@@ -93,6 +93,7 @@

if "$BINDIR"/vmware-modconfig --appname="VMware Workstation" --icon="vmware-workstation" &amp;&amp;
/sbin/modinfo vmmon; then
+ export LD_LIBRARY_PATH=/usr/lib/vmware/lib/libcurl.so.4
exec "$libdir"/bin/"vmware" "$@"
fi
```
